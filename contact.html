Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a service visit?

You can schedule a visit by filling out the form above, calling our 24/7 hotline, or sending us a quick message on WhatsApp. We typically confirm appointments within 1 hour.

What areas do you currently serve?

We provide water purifier installation, repair, and maintenance services across the entire Blue City Metro region and suburbs within a 20km radius.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes! We offer a 30-day service warranty on all repair jobs. Additionally, any spare parts replaced come with their respective manufacturer's warranty.

How often should I service my water purifier?

For optimal water quality and machine longevity, we recommend a general service every 3-4 months and a complete filter change once a year, depending on your water usage.

Are your technicians certified?

Absolutely. All our technicians undergo rigorous training and background checks to ensure they provide safe, professional, and expert service in your home.
